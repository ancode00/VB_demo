Botwot Virtual Assistant - Knowledge Base
 1.⁠ ⁠What is Botwot?
Botwot is an AI-powered virtual assistant platform that helps businesses automate customer support, lead qualification, and task management using conversational AI.

 2.⁠ ⁠What are the key features of Botwot?

Natural language understanding and generation

Omnichannel support (Web, WhatsApp, Telephony, etc.)

Integration with CRM and other business tools

Automated appointment scheduling

Lead capture and qualification

24/7 support capability

Customizable conversation flows

Analytics dashboard for monitoring performance

 3.⁠ ⁠How does Botwot integrate with other tools?
Botwot can be integrated with popular CRMs (like Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho), ticketing systems, and calendar tools (Google Calendar, Outlook). Integration is done via APIs and no-code connectors.

 4.⁠ ⁠How can I get started with Botwot?

Sign up on the Botwot platform

Follow the onboarding wizard to connect your channels and CRM

Customize your assistant’s conversation flows using the visual editor

Launch and monitor conversations from the dashboard

 5.⁠ ⁠What channels does Botwot support?
Botwot supports:

Website chat widgets

WhatsApp and SMS

Facebook Messenger

Telephony (outgoing/incoming calls)

Custom integrations via API

 6.⁠ ⁠How does Botwot help qualify leads?
Botwot asks qualifying questions, captures lead information, and syncs it directly to your CRM. The bot can assign lead scores and notify your team in real time.

 7.⁠ ⁠Is Botwot secure and compliant?
Yes, Botwot uses industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest. It supports GDPR compliance and role-based access control for enterprise customers.

 8.⁠ ⁠How can I customize my Botwot assistant’s responses?
You can use the Botwot dashboard’s flow builder to edit messages, set rules, and add FAQs. The platform also supports dynamic variables to personalize replies.

 9.⁠ ⁠How does Botwot handle escalations to humans?
If a customer query is too complex, Botwot can transfer the conversation to a human agent via live chat or email. The handover is seamless and preserves chat context.

10.⁠ ⁠Who can I contact for Botwot support?
You can email support@botwot.com or use the in-app help chat for immediate assistance.

11.⁠ ⁠What’s the pricing model for Botwot?
Botwot offers monthly and annual subscription plans based on number of users, channels, and volume of conversations. For detailed pricing, visit www.botwot.com/pricing or contact sales.

12.⁠ ⁠Can Botwot be trained for specific industries?
Yes, Botwot supports industry-specific templates (e.g., healthcare, real estate, e-commerce) to accelerate setup and provide relevant automation out of the box.
